Let's Ride!

How Does This Work?

In today’s fast-paced world, you’ve got places you need to be but not enough time in your jam-packed schedule. Wouldn’t it be nice if there were a transportation service that changed the way you get around daily? Introducing Joyride, an innovative transportation service that helps people get to their destination faster and hassle-free. Getting started is as easy as tapping a button in our app.

Download The App

Head over to the app store and download the Joyride app. You’re just a few taps away from getting your ride.

Request A Shuttle

We can get you there. Simply request a ride on the app and we'll connect you with a shuttle immediately.

Pick A Price

Pick the valuation you're willing to pay and your ride is set up. The only surge is the acceleration you get when taking off.

Ride Off To Success!

After matching with your shuttle and agreeing to its terms, you’ll be all set. Your shuttle will arrive shortly to pick you up.

Our Story

Joyride was founded by a group of three students from Yeshiva University. The idea for Joyride was born out of a desire to provide efficient and reliable transportation solutions. Inspired by the need for faster, more flexible transportation options, they saw an opportunity to create an on-demand transportation app that connects users with shuttles. Using their expertise in software engineering and a passion for innovation, Joyride was formed and has since raised significant venture capital. Today, Joyride has a growing network of shuttles fulfilling hundreds of rides each day.
